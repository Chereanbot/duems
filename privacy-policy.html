1. Introduction

Welcome to the Dilla University Employee Management System ("EMS"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our EMS.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By accessing or using the EMS, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Employee ID and position
  • Department and supervisor information
  • Attendance and leave records
  • Performance evaluation data
  • Training and certification records

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the EMS, we automatically collect:

  • Device information
  • Log data
  • Usage information
  • Location data (when enabled)

3. Information Usage

We use the collected information for:

  • Managing employee records
  • Processing attendance and leave requests
  • Conducting performance evaluations
  • Generating reports and analytics
  • Improving the EMS functionality
  • Ensuring system security

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • University administrators
  • Department supervisors
  • HR personnel
  • Service providers
  • Legal authorities when required

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage
  • Regular backups

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Export your data

7. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Maintain your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze system usage
  • Improve system performance

8. Children's Privacy

The EMS is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Attendance Management

Learn how to manage employee attendance, including tracking, reporting, and managing attendance records.

Attendance Tracking Methods

The system supports multiple attendance tracking methods:

  • Biometric devices
  • Web-based check-in/check-out
  • Mobile app check-in
  • Manual entry by administrators
Note: The system automatically calculates work hours based on check-in and check-out times.

Managing Attendance Records

Common attendance management tasks:

  • Viewing daily attendance records
  • Editing attendance entries
  • Approving attendance corrections
  • Managing overtime records
  • Generating attendance reports
Warning: Always verify the reason for attendance corrections before approving them.

Attendance Policies

Configure attendance policies for:

  • Working hours
  • Break times
  • Late arrival thresholds
  • Overtime rules
  • Holiday schedules
Tip: You can set up automatic notifications for late arrivals and absences.

Attendance Reports

Available attendance reports include:

  • Daily attendance summary
  • Monthly attendance report
  • Late arrival report
  • Overtime report
  • Department-wise attendance
Note: Reports can be exported in various formats (PDF, Excel, CSV) and scheduled for automatic generation.

Attendance Analytics

The system provides analytics for:

  • Attendance trends
  • Punctuality metrics
  • Overtime patterns
  • Department comparison
  • Attendance compliance
Tip: Use analytics to identify patterns and improve attendance management strategies.

Departments

Learn how to manage departments and organizational structure in the system.

Department Structure

The system supports a hierarchical department structure:

  • Main departments (e.g., Academic, Administrative)
  • Sub-departments
  • Teams within departments
Note: The department structure affects reporting relationships and access permissions.

Creating a Department

  1. Navigate to the Departments section
  2. Click "Add Department"
  3. Fill in the department details:
    • Department name
    • Department code
    • Parent department (if applicable)
    • Department head
    • Description
  4. Configure department settings:
    • Budget allocation
    • Reporting structure
    • Access permissions
  5. Click "Save" to create the department

Managing Departments

Common department management tasks:

  • Editing department information
  • Assigning department heads
  • Managing department members
  • Setting department budgets
  • Configuring reporting relationships
Warning: Changes to department structure may affect reporting relationships and access permissions.

Department Reports

Available department reports include:

  • Department headcount
  • Budget allocation and utilization
  • Performance metrics
  • Attendance statistics
  • Leave utilization
Tip: You can schedule regular department reports to be automatically generated and sent to department heads.

About Dilla University

A leading institution of higher education in Ethiopia, committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community service. Our university stands as a beacon of knowledge and innovation in the region.

Our Mission Our History

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education, conduct problem-solving research, and render need-based community services through competent professionals who can contribute to the development of the country. We strive to create an environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Vision

To be one of the leading universities in Ethiopia by 2030, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement. We envision a future where our graduates are leaders in their fields, driving positive change in society.

Our History

1996

Dilla University was established as a college under Debub University, marking the beginning of our journey in higher education.

2004

Gained autonomous status and became an independent university, allowing us to expand our academic programs and research initiatives.

2010

Expanded academic programs and infrastructure significantly, establishing new faculties and research centers to meet growing educational demands.

2020

Celebrated 20 years of excellence in education and research, marking our commitment to academic excellence and community development.

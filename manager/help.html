Frequently Asked Questions

To add a new employee, go to the Employees page and click the "Add Employee" button. Fill in the required information in the form and submit. The new employee will receive an email with their login credentials.

Navigate to the Schedule Management page. You can create, edit, or delete schedules for individual employees or groups. Use the calendar view to manage weekly schedules and special arrangements.

Go to the Attendance Management page to view attendance records. You can mark absences, approve late arrivals, and generate attendance reports. For specific issues, use the "Report Issue" feature.

Access the Leave Requests page to view pending requests. You can approve, reject, or request more information. The system will automatically notify employees of your decision.

Visit the Reports page to access various report templates. Select the type of report, set the date range, and choose the format (PDF, Excel, or CSV). Click "Generate" to create the report.

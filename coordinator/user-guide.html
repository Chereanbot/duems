Getting Started

Welcome to the Dilla University Employee Management System. This guide will help you navigate and use the system effectively.

System Requirements
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • Internet connection
  • Valid login credentials
Logging In

To access the system:

  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. Click the "Login" button
Tip: If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
Navigation

The system has a consistent layout across all pages:

  • Sidebar: Contains main navigation links and quick actions
  • Header: Shows notifications, user profile, and mobile menu toggle
  • Main Content: Displays the content of the selected page
Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of your work status and quick access to important features.

Dashboard Components
  • Welcome Section: Displays your name and profile picture
  • Quick Stats: Shows your current status, weekly hours, next shift, and leave balance
  • Upcoming Schedule: Lists your upcoming shifts
  • Recent Attendance: Shows your recent clock-in and clock-out records
  • Quick Actions: Provides buttons for common tasks like clocking in/out and requesting leave
  • Recent Notifications: Displays important updates and alerts
Using Quick Actions

The Quick Actions section allows you to perform common tasks directly from the dashboard:

  1. Click the "Quick Actions" button in the welcome section
  2. Select the desired action from the modal
  3. Fill in the required information
  4. Click the appropriate button to complete the action
Tip: You can also access these actions from the sidebar or through the respective pages.
My Profile

The profile page displays your personal and professional information.

Profile Sections
  • Basic Information: Contains your personal details like name, ID, gender, contact information
  • Role Information: Shows your department, assigned team, shift type, and manager
  • Permissions: Lists the actions you're authorized to perform in the system
Updating Your Profile

To update your profile information:

  1. Navigate to the Profile page
  2. Click the "Edit Profile" button
  3. Update the desired information in the modal
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile
Note: Some fields may be read-only and cannot be edited. Contact your administrator if you need to change these fields.
Work Schedule

The schedule page allows you to view and manage your work schedule.

Viewing Your Schedule

The schedule is displayed in two formats:

  • Calendar View: Shows your shifts in a monthly calendar format
  • List View: Displays your upcoming shifts in a table format
Managing Your Schedule

As a coordinator, you can:

  • View your personal schedule
  • View and manage your team's schedule
  • Request shift swaps
  • Add notes to shifts
Requesting a Shift Swap

To request a shift swap:

  1. Navigate to the Schedule page
  2. Find the shift you want to swap
  3. Click the "Request Swap" button
  4. Select a colleague to swap with
  5. Add a reason for the swap request
  6. Click "Submit Request"
Tip: Shift swap requests must be approved by your manager before they take effect.
Attendance

The attendance page allows you to manage your clock-in and clock-out records.

Recording Attendance

To clock in or out:

  1. Click the "Clock In" or "Clock Out" button in the Quick Actions section
  2. Verify the current time and date
  3. Add any optional notes
  4. Click "Confirm Clock In" or "Confirm Clock Out"
Viewing Attendance Records

The attendance page displays:

  • Your clock-in and clock-out times
  • Total hours worked
  • Attendance status (Present, Late, Absent)
  • Any notes or comments
Managing Team Attendance

As a coordinator, you can:

  • View your team's attendance records
  • Update attendance records if needed
  • Generate attendance reports
Note: Attendance records are used for payroll and compliance purposes. Ensure accurate clock-in and clock-out times.
Leave Requests

The leave requests page allows you to request time off and manage your leave balance.

Requesting Leave

To submit a leave request:

  1. Navigate to the Leave Requests page or use the Quick Actions button
  2. Click "New Leave Request"
  3. Select the leave type (Annual, Sick, Personal, Emergency)
  4. Choose the start and end dates
  5. Provide a reason for the leave
  6. Add contact information during leave (optional)
  7. Click "Submit Request"
Managing Leave Requests

The leave requests page displays:

  • Your current leave balance
  • Pending leave requests
  • Approved and rejected leave requests
  • Leave history
Approving Team Leave Requests

As a coordinator, you can:

  • View leave requests from your team members
  • Approve or reject leave requests
  • Add comments to leave requests
Tip: Submit leave requests as early as possible to ensure proper coverage and approval.
Team Management

The team management section allows you to manage your assigned team members.

Viewing Your Team

To view your team members:

  1. Navigate to the My Team page
  2. View the list of assigned team members
  3. Click on a team member to view their details
Managing Shift Swaps

To approve or reject shift swap requests:

  1. Navigate to the Shift Swaps page
  2. View pending swap requests
  3. Review the request details
  4. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
  5. Add comments if needed
Adding Performance Notes

To add performance notes for team members:

  1. Navigate to the Performance Notes page
  2. Select a team member
  3. Click "Add Note"
  4. Enter the performance details
  5. Select the note type (Positive, Improvement, Issue)
  6. Click "Save Note"
Note: Performance notes are visible to managers and may be used for evaluations.
Reports

The reports section allows you to generate and view various reports.

Available Reports
  • Attendance Reports: Shows attendance records and statistics
  • Performance Reports: Displays team performance metrics
  • Leave Reports: Shows leave usage and balances
  • Shift Reports: Displays shift coverage and distribution
Generating Reports

To generate a report:

  1. Navigate to the desired report page
  2. Set the report parameters (date range, department, team, etc.)
  3. Click "Generate Report"
  4. View the report results
  5. Optionally, export the report in various formats
Exporting Reports

To export a report:

  1. Generate the report
  2. Click the "Export" button
  3. Select the export format (Excel, PDF, CSV)
  4. Choose what to include in the export
  5. Click "Export" to download the file
Tip: Reports can be scheduled to be generated and emailed automatically on a regular basis.
Notifications

The notifications system keeps you informed about important updates and events.

Types of Notifications
  • System Notifications: Updates about the system
  • Schedule Notifications: Changes to your schedule
  • Leave Notifications: Updates about leave requests
  • Team Notifications: Updates about your team
Viewing Notifications

To view your notifications:

  1. Click the bell icon in the header
  2. View the notifications dropdown
  3. Click on a notification to view details
  4. Click "View All" to see all notifications
Notification Settings

To manage your notification settings:

  1. Go to your profile settings
  2. Select the "Notifications" tab
  3. Choose which types of notifications you want to receive
  4. Select your preferred notification method (email, in-app, both)
  5. Click "Save Settings"
Help & Support

The help and support section provides assistance when you need it.

Accessing Help

To get help:

  1. Navigate to the Help Center page
  2. Browse the FAQs or knowledge base articles
  3. Search for specific topics
  4. Contact support if needed
Submitting Support Tickets

To submit a support ticket:

  1. Go to the Help Center page
  2. Click "New Support Ticket"
  3. Select the issue category
  4. Provide a detailed description of the problem
  5. Attach any relevant files
  6. Click "Submit Ticket"
Contacting Support

You can contact support through:

  • Email: support@dillauniversity.edu
  • Phone: +251 911 123 456
  • Support hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Tip: For urgent issues, use the "Notify Manager" feature to directly inform your manager.
Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.

If you forget to clock in or out, notify your manager immediately. They can update your attendance record manually.

To request a shift swap, go to the Schedule page, find the shift you want to swap, click "Request Swap," select a colleague, and submit the request.

Your leave balance is displayed on the dashboard and in the Leave Requests page. You can also view detailed leave history in the Leave Reports section.

To report an issue to your manager, go to the Notify Manager page, select the notification type, priority level, provide details, and submit the notification.

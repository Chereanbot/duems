You can manage your team's schedule through the Schedule page. There you can view, create, and modify shifts, assign team members, and handle shift swaps.

To submit a leave request, go to the Leave Requests page and click on "New Leave Request". Fill in the required details and submit for approval.

You can report issues to your manager through the Notify Manager page. Select the appropriate notification type, priority level, and provide detailed information about the issue.
